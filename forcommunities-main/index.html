A little about For Communities

Our company was incepted in 2011. Our services company, For Communities Pty Ltd was incorporated in 2020 to serve communities.

We purpose a portion of our profits to the For Communities Fund, which grants funding for youth entrepreneurship in Gauteng and its surroundings. We have funded the creation of 10 start-ups to date.
